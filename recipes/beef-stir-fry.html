Beef Stir-Fry Recipe

Description

Quick and easy beef stir-fry, prepped and ready in less than 30 minutes! This dish utilizes ingredients most people already have in their pantries, but it's very flexible, so you can add/subtract ingredients if you have extra or don't have enough. Served with rice or lo mein noodles, this will be the best beef stir-fry you've ever made (if you've never had stir-fry before).

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil - 2 Tablespoons
  • Beef Sirloin - 1 pound, cut into 2-inch strips
  • Broccoli Florets - 1 cup
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, cut into strips
  • 2 Carrots, thinly sliced
  • 1 Green Onion, chopped
  • Minced Garlic - 1 Teaspoon
  • Soy sauce - 2 Tablespoons

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir beef until browned, 3 to 4 minutes.
  2. Move beef to the side of the wok and add broccoli, bell pepper, carrots, green onion, and garlic to the center of the wok. Cook and stir vegetables for 2 minutes.
  3. Stir beef into vegetables and season with soy sauce and sesame seeds. Continue to cook and stir until vegetables are tender, about 2 more minutes.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
